Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Welcome to **EasyConnect**. This Privacy Policy explains how our application ecosystem collects, uses, protects, and discloses personal information when you use the EasyConnect mobile app and its connected remote web dashboard. Our primary goal is to provide a safe, accessible, and simple communication system for elderly, low-literacy, or non-literate users while ensuring their personal data is treated with the highest standard of security.

1. Information We Access & Collect

To enable the voice, video, SOS, and large-photo dialer features, our app requests and accesses several permissions and data points. Below is a detailed breakdown of what we access and why:

  • Contacts Data (READ_CONTACTS, WRITE_CONTACTS): The app accesses your local device contacts to display them as large, tapable photos in the visual grid, read names out loud using Text-to-Speech (TTS), and identify incoming callers. Contacts are synchronized securely with the Firebase Firestore database under your family's private sync code to allow remote contact additions and reordering via this Web Dashboard.
  • Device Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): Location permissions are used strictly when the SOS Emergency feature is triggered. If enabled in your settings, the app queries the device's GPS coordinates at the moment of the SOS countdown and compiles a Google Maps link to send to your designates. Location tracking does NOT run continuously in the background, nor is it uploaded to external servers.
  • Microphone & Audio Recording (RECORD_AUDIO): The app accesses the microphone to allow caregivers to record custom voice names for contact cards (e.g. saying "Mom" or "Doctor"). These audio recordings are stored locally and synced with Firebase Storage.
  • Camera & Storage (CAMERA, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): We access the camera and media gallery to let you capture or choose profile images for contact cards, which are synced with Firebase Storage.
  • Phone Calling & State (CALL_PHONE, READ_PHONE_STATE, MANAGE_OWN_CALLS): The app requires dialer privileges to initiate calls, answer incoming system calls instantly, and overlay calling information when set as the device's Default Dialer.

2. How We Use and Share Information

EasyConnect handles your data with strict confidentiality. Specifically:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or trade your contact listings, location details, audio files, or profile photos to advertising networks, analytics companies, or any other third parties.
  • Scoped Family Syncing: All contact cards, settings, photos, and voice labels are securely synced and compartmentalized in your family's dedicated space using Firestore and Firebase Storage, protected by a unique Family Sync Code.

3. Data Security & Retention

All data transmitted between the mobile application, the Firebase servers, and this web administration dashboard is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS (HTTPS). Photos and audio are stored securely on Google's cloud infrastructure.

Caregivers can permanently delete all contacts and uploaded data from the cloud at any time by selecting the **"Wipe Family Cloud Database"** option in the footer of the admin web dashboard.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to delete it immediately.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or Play Store policy updates. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy or data security practices, please feel free to reach out to our team at your primary support address.
